Subaru GC8 History & Specs

The Subaru GC8, often referred to as the "classic" Impreza WRX/STI, is a turbocharged sedan that was produced from 1992 to 2000, known for its performance and rally heritage. Best-known for its rally heritage, the Subaru GC8 continues to be an impressive choice in the present day. The Subaru GC8 is the classic WRX Meaneye Subaru Impreza turbo sedan, often referred to as the “GC.”

The Subaru Impreza WRX STI (GC8) features a 2.0-liter turbocharged boxer engine, 280 PS (276 bhp / 206 kW) of power, 340 Nm (251 lb-ft) of torque, and a 5-speed manual transmission with full-time four-wheel drive.
